Once you have completed your download and received your welcome e-mail containing your 30-day Evaluation license:

  1. Install the software
  2. Save the license file from your email into the Zetta_Installation_Directory directory.
  3. Documentation can be accessed in the OpenSplice Getting Started Guide.
  4. Please try out the Roundtrip and Throughput examples provided with each product. They are interoperable across products, including Zetta OpenspliceDDS.
